About Me

I operate at the intersection of HR Technology, Automation, and Process Strategy.

With a technical development background, I bridge the gap between complex architecture and human-centric workflows. My focus is on scalability and measurable impact—moving HR from manual tasks to automated, data-driven systems.

I earned my degree in Human Resource Development Management from the Polytechnic University of the Philippines, where I built a strong foundation in people strategy and organizational development. Early in my career, I became drawn to the operational side of HR—the systems, workflows, and data that enable the function to scale and perform. This led me to transition from traditional HR roles into technical specialization, where I have spent the past decade working on process automation, ETL and data integration, HRIS administration, and governance.

To further strengthen my expertise and deepen the connection between business and technology, I am currently pursuing a Master of Science in Information Technology at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines.
